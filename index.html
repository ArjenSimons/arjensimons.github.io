Vittles
android dev
Kotlin
Vittles is an app that helps you reduce food waste. You can scan the barcode and expiration date of a product using your camera. The app will send you a notification when your food is almost expired. The app also contains a report that shows how much food you wasted and saved. Vittles was developed by a team of six students for a school project.

Java
Bubbles Unite! is a bubble shooter in which you must mix two colours to shoot a colour of your choosing.
