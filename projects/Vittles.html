Vittles
Project Type Group project | 6 students
Project Timeline 16 weeks | 2019-2020
Software Used Android Studio
Languages Used Kotlin, XML

Vittles is an app that helps you reduce food waste. You can scan the barcode and expiration date of a product using your camera. The app will send you a notification when your food is almost expired. The app also contains a report that shows how much food you wasted and saved.
