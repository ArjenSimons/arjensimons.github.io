Edu Play Book
Project Type Internship | 4-8 developers
Project Timeline 20 weeks | 2019
Software Used Unity
Languages Used C#

Edu Play Book is a children’s books reading app. It contains original stories for children to read and listen to. The app also contains numerous educational minigames that help children improve their math, language, problem solving and other skills. I worked on Edu Play Book for half a year during my internship at DTT.
